Copernicus


This app is designed to display sunrise and sunset times and aid in astronomy.


It has been designed using Flask and custom HTML. Astral calculations are done using Skyfield.

Currently the Sunrise module is the only one completed and in production.

It charts sunrise and sunset times across the year and on a selected date for a selected location.

Twilight phases and their times are also included.

This module is a re-implementation from scratch of the excellent Sunrise calculator provided by timeanddate.com.
